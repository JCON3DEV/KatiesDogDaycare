Katies Dog Daycare and Boarding

Reliable and flexible home from home service looking after your dogs while you work or go on holiday. I offer short term or long term stays, ad hoc visits or anything else you require.

I have looked after clients dogs for the last 10 years and I absolutely love what I do and like to help clients as much as I can by making sure I am available and flexible to their requirements and ensuring their dogs are happy and well looked after and exercised.

The dogs love coming to me and when many owners tell their dogs they are going to "Katie's the dogs sit and wait by the front door waiting for me! Hence the name" Katies"!

I offer daycare so always have several dogs that travel and walk all together as a pack, which the dogs love as lots of playmates and social interaction and because they are well exercised together they all sleep well too!

I am available 7 days a week 52 weeks of the year including Christmas and Easter, etc!

I live in ripley and am happy to collect locally but also happy for clients to drop their dogs off.
Daytime hours are from 6.15am to 9pm so dogs can come and go anytime between then so that can accommodate most peoples working hours!

Prices: daycare whether it is one hour or all day is £15 and overnight boarding is £20. Very short notice can be accommodated.
